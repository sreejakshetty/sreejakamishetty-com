About Me

Tech Lead at Google Chrome with 11+ years in product, driving Agentic AI and GenAI features like Compose and Tab Organization. I founded and scaled Chromium GSoC to 75+ projects and 1,000+ contributors, and was honoured with Google's P&E Excellence Award. Speaker at GHC'22.

Grew up in Hyderabad in an entrepreneurial family that instilled the values I lead by: growth mindset, resilience, and service to community. My love of technology sparked with the iPhone 3 in 2008. I went on to study CS & ML at IIIT Hyderabad, publish research, and file a patent — all driven by a belief in tech's power to change lives.

Tennis and cricket since age 10 shaped how I think about teamwork and leadership. Vipassana meditation and solo travel clarified my purpose: build technology that makes the world more equitable. As an angel investor, I back founders who share that conviction.
